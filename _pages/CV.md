## Research Activities  
**Research Student**  (April 2016 – August 2020)  
**McGill University Health Center (Cedars Cancer Center)**  
Montreal, Québec, Canada
* Helped explain the limited effectiveness of intravascular brachytherapy for treatment of coronary artery disease using Monte Carlo particle physics simulations. 
* Developed a toolkit to determine patient-specific microdosimetric distributions using information derived from pathology slides of patient’s tissues.
* Supervised by Prof. Shirin Enger.  

**Visiting Researcher** (May 2017 – July 2017)  
**Danish Technical University, Risø: Center for Nuclear Technology**  
Risø, Denmark

- Supervised by Prof. Lars Lindvold.  

**Visiting Researcher** (July 2018 – August 2018)   
**Uppsala University Hospital**  
Uppsala, Sweden

**Graduate Researcher** (August 2020 – May 2025)  
**The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center, Department of Radiation Physics**  
Houston, Texas, United States of America
- I developed models to determine the biological effectiveness of proton therapy under the supervision of Dr. Radhe Mohan.
- Developed SuperTrack, a GPU-accelerated software for the rapid calculation of microdosimetric spectra; which was later incorporated into RayStation-IonPG 2023B.  

**Medical Physics Resident**  (July 2025 – Present)

Seattle, Washington, United States of America

- In addition to clinical training I am involved in FLASH radiobiology related research with Dr. Clemens Grassberger and investigating treatment outcomes following intravascular brachytherapy with Dr. Kent Wallner.

## Clinical Activities
**Machine calibration and quality assurance**  
- Performed monthly and annual quality assurance measurements in accordance with AAPM TG-142 and medical physics practice guidelines on an Elekta Infinity, Varian Ethos, Varian TrueBeam, and General Electric LightSpeed CT scanner.
- Regularly recalibrate diodes used for in-vivo monitoring of megavoltage photon and electron therapy.
Surface guidance
- Regularly provide technical troubleshooting assistance and isocenter/radiation isocenter calibrations for Varian IDENTIFY surface-guidance camera system.
Intravascular brachytherapy
- Experience performing seed assays, surveys, daily QA, and covering intravascular brachytherapy procedures provided in the cath-lab with a Novoste Beta Cath 3.5F device.  

**Patient-specific quality assurance**  
- Perform patient-specific quality assurance using a Sun Nuclear ArcCheck, Mobius FX, and Sun Nuclear Fraction0 log-file based analysis for 300+ IMRT/VMAT/SRS/SBRT plans annually.  

**Treatment planning**  
- Plan on the order of 50 total body irradiation (TBI) treatments per year for myeloablative as well as non-myeloablative stem cell transplant conditioning.
- Plan  on the order of 10 3D conformal radiation therapy or intensity-modulated radiation therapy cases annually using the RayStation treatment planning system.  

**Device commissioning**  
- Commissioned, calibrated, and baselined a Sun Nuclear Daily QA3 and ICProfiler for daily and monthly quality assurance measurements, respectively on an Elekta C-arm linear accelerator.
